How to use
NutriSnap Guide
🚀 Quick start in 3 steps
1️⃣ Complete onboarding to set your calorie goal
2️⃣ Tap the green + button to log food
3️⃣ Check your ring and ask the Coach anytime
🏠 Home screen
🔵
Calorie ring
Big circle showing calories eaten vs your daily goal. Green = good, amber = nearly there, red = over limit.
💊
Macro tiles
4 tiles below the ring showing Protein, Carbs, Fat, Fiber. Each has a mini bar filling up as you log food.
💧
Water tracker
Tap each 💧 cup to log water intake. Goal is 8 cups a day. Tap a filled cup to undo it.
🔥
Streak badge
Top right. Counts days in a row you've logged food. Miss a day and it resets to 0 — keep it going!
🍽️
Today's log
All meals logged today, newest first. Tap the ✕ on any entry to delete it. Purple "AI" badge means it was scanned by AI.
➕ Adding food (green + button)
📷
AI photo scan ⭐ best
Take a photo or upload any food image. AI identifies the dish and estimates calories, protein, carbs, fat. Review the result then tap "+ Add to Log".
✏️
Manual entry
Scroll down in the + panel. Type food name, calories, and macros yourself. Good for known meals.
⚡
Quick presets
Chips for common foods — Banana, Dal, Roti, Idli, Samosa, Paneer, Chicken, Rice and more. One tap to add instantly.
📊 Insights tab
📈
Weekly calorie chart
Bar chart of your calories for each day this week. Today's bar is highlighted in green.
🥧
Macro donut
Pie chart showing how today's calories are split between protein, carbs and fat.
💡
Smart insights
Auto-generated tips based on your data — low protein warnings, streak celebrations, hydration reminders and more.
🤖 Coach tab
💬
AI nutrition coach unique
Chat with Claude AI. It knows everything you've logged today and gives personalised advice in real time.
⚡
Suggestion chips
Tap chips like "Am I on track?", "What should I eat next?" or "How's my protein?" for instant answers.
💡
What to ask
Try: "Give me a 500 kcal meal idea", "Analyse my week", "Am I eating too much fat?", "What's a good snack for muscle gain?"
👤 Profile tab
🎯
Edit goals
Change your daily calorie, protein and carb targets anytime. Tap the number and type a new value.
🔄
Restart onboarding
Redo the full setup — recalculates your BMR and gives you a fresh plan based on new weight or goals.
💡 Good to know
📱
Install as app
iPhone: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". Android: tap ⋮ → "Add to Home Screen". Opens fullscreen like a native app!
💾
Auto-save
Everything saves automatically in your browser. Your streak and goals persist across days. Each day starts fresh.
🌙
Dark mode only
NutriSnap uses a pure black OLED-friendly dark theme — easy on your eyes and battery at night.
🔑 The AI features (photo scan + coach) use your Anthropic API credits. Each food scan costs roughly ₹0.10–0.20. Your $5 credit = ~500+ scans.
